About Me

Hi I’m Josh, I’m a designer / developer from Wellington. I believe that something can be learned from every experience and that great design is about improving experiences.

Over the last few years I have designed websites, managed projects, solved backend bugs and improved usability for a number small businesses throughout NZ. Each of these experiences presented new challenges and have helped me to become a more decisive and efficient designer.

I would say that I am an energetic person, someone who likes to stay busy and hates doing nothing. I am also someone who enjoys trying new things and being self sufficient in those ventures. I come from a large family of diverse cultures and enjoy traveling, live music and coaching basketball in my spare time.
